    摄影师简介

    贾方

    建筑摄影家、影像艺术家，曾就读于南京艺术学院和北京电影学院，拥有摄影艺术方向的学士和硕士学位。现担任南京艺术学院摄影系副教授、系主任；中国文化部《建筑与文化》杂志“建筑与摄影”栏目主持人、《ID+C》杂志摄影特约撰稿人；中国国家艺术基金《剧院建筑》项目主持人；2019平遥国际摄影大展国际教育论坛学术主持人；2019“OPPO全球探索计划”摄影导师；金拴马桩奖、中国摄影“伯奇杯”、1839摄影奖、中国摄影毕业季“优秀作品奖”、鸣凤堂中日青年影像艺术节、PhotoSpeaks中韩摄影展的推委、评委。

    贾方建筑摄影工作室创建于2004年，工作室与国内外多家知名设计机构、地产集团保持合作，并协助合作机构多次获得国际重要设计奖项。工作室的主要摄影题材涉及建筑、景观、照明、软装、酒店、地产、商业、办公、餐厅、工厂等。贾方的建筑摄影作品多次刊登在《DOMOS》(意大利)、《商店建筑》（日本）、《中国建筑装饰与装修》、《DI设计新潮》、《ID+C室内设计与装修》、《中国摄影》、《时尚家居》、《现代广告》、《美国室内中文版》、等杂志。曾编著出版了《大师智慧》、《惊艳米兰》、《建筑归来》、《宴遇》系列、《盛世大堂》等图书18部。撰写建筑摄影研究文章20余篇；艺术作品曾在英国、韩国、日本以及中国的平遥国际摄影大展、丽水国际摄影节、济南国际摄影展、北京国际摄影周、中国摄影艺术节、上海美术馆、南京艺术学院美术馆等地展出。

    Photographer Profile

    Jia Fang

    An Architectural Photographer、Image Artist. He achieved Bachelor's Degree in Photography Art from Nanjing University of the Arts and Master's Degree in Photography Art from Beijing Film Academy.Now, he acts as an associate professor and director of Photography Department in Nanjing University of the Arts；He is also the host of “Architecture and Photography” column from the magazine 《Architecture and Culture》 which is run by Ministry of Culture of China; the staff writer of photography of 《ID+C》Magazine; the host of 《Theater Architecture》program of China  National Arts Fund; the academic host of international education forum at PINGYAO International Photography Festival in 2019;the photography mentor of “OPPO Global Exploration Program ”in 2019.He is also the judge of Golden Shuanmazhuang Awards、“BOQI CUP ”of China Photography、1839 Photography Awards、“Outstanding Works Award” of China Photography in Graduation Season、MEIHUDO International Youth Visual Media Festival and Photo Speaks China-South Korea Photography Exhibition.

    Jia Fang Architectural Photography Studio was founded in 2004 which maintained a good partnership with well-known design agencies and real estate corporations from both domestic and abroad. In addition, they have helped the partners achieve many major international design awards. The major photography subjects of Jia Fang Architectural Photography Studio include：architecture、 landscape、lighting、soft outfit、hotel、real estate、business、office、 restaurant、factory etc. Jia Fang’s architectural photography works are published in the 《DOMOS》(Italy)、《Shop Design&Interior Design》(Japan)、《China Building Design&Decoration》、《DI Design Trends 》、《ID+C Interior Design+Construction》、《Chinese Photography》、《Trends Home》、《Modern Advertisement》and《Interior Design China》for many times. Jia Fang edited and published 18 books， Including Designs by Masters、Stunning Milan、Architecture Return、 Restaurant Space series、Grand Lobby etc. He also wrote more than 20 articles about architectural photography studies. His artistic works were exhibited in Britain、South Korea、Japan and PINGYAO International Photography Festival、LISHUI International Photography Festival、JINAN International Photography Festival、PHOTO BEIJING 、China Photography Art Festival、Shanghai Art Museum and Art Museum of Nanjing University of the Arts.
